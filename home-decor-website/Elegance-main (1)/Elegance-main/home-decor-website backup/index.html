About Elegance Home Decor

Welcome to Elegance Home Decor, where we believe that every home deserves to reflect the beauty and personality of its inhabitants. Since our founding, we have been dedicated to curating the finest collection of home decor pieces that combine timeless elegance with modern design sensibilities.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to transform ordinary living spaces into extraordinary sanctuaries. We work with skilled artisans and designers from around the world to bring you unique pieces that tell a story. Each item in our collection is carefully selected for its quality, craftsmanship, and ability to enhance any interior.

At Elegance, we understand that home decor is more than just accessories – it's about creating an atmosphere that nurtures and inspires. Whether you're looking for statement pieces or subtle accents, our curated collection offers something for every style and space.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, returns, and more.

How long does shipping take?

Standard orders take 3-7 business days within India. Express shipping is 1-3 days. International shipping 5-12 days.

What is your return policy?

30-day returns on unused items. Pre-paid labels provided. Custom orders are final sale.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, worldwide shipping starting at ₹1,999 with tracking. Delivery 5-12 business days.

Are your products AR enabled?

Yes! Scan the QR code on product pages to view items in AR before purchasing.

How do I track my order?

Tracking number sent via email & SMS. You can also check My Orders section in your account.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit/Debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), PayPal, UPI, Net Banking, Wallets.

Is there a minimum order value?

No minimum order value. Free shipping on orders above ₹5,000.

How do I care for my decor pieces?

Dust regularly with microfiber cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals. Specific care instructions included with each order.
